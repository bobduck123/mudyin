## What Is Mudyin?

Mudyin is a safe, moderated community where young people can:
- Share their experiences in healing programs (YSMP, Thrive Tribe, Healing Centre)
- Upload photos and create stories
- Connect with other members
- Celebrate achievements and milestones
- Receive peer support

The platform has:
- **Age-appropriate content filters** (no adult-only content)
- **24/7 moderation** by trained volunteers
- **Crisis resources** (mental health hotlines)
- **Parent access** to activity (what you approve)
- **Anti-bullying protections** (strict enforcement)

## Your Child's Account

### What Can Your Child Do?

✅ **Upload photos** from program activities and events

## Safety & Moderation

### Our Protections

1. **Content Filtering**
   - Automated system scans all posts
   - Flags potentially harmful content
   - Human review by trained moderators
   - Removal within 24 hours if needed

2. **Child Safety Team**
   - Specialized training in working with minors
   - Background checks completed
   - Mandatory reporting requirements
   - 24/7 monitoring

3. **Crisis Support**
   - Mental health hotlines available
   - Immediate notification to you if mentioned
   - Rapid escalation to authorities if needed
   - Crisis resources displayed on platform

4. **Anti-Bullying**
   - Zero-tolerance for bullying
   - Reporting tools for your child
   - Immediate moderation action
   - Consequences for bullies (warnings, bans)

### What Happens If Rules Are Broken

| Violation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| **First offense** | Warning + education message |
| **Second offense** | Content removed + 7-day ban |
| **Third offense** | 30-day ban |
| **Serious violations** | Permanent ban + notify authorities |

## Parental Guidance

### Talking with Your Child

**Before they join**:
- Explain what Mudyin is and how it works
- Discuss digital safety and responsibility
- Review Community Guidelines together
- Explain what information is private
- Discuss what they might encounter

**While they're on the platform**:
- Check weekly activity summary
- Ask them about their posts and connections
- Discuss any concerns or strange interactions
- Model positive digital behavior
- Keep communication open and non-judgmental

**If problems arise**:
- Don't panic - reach out to Mudyin staff
- Encourage your child to report harassment
- Talk with them about what happened
- Work with moderation team on resolution
- Monitor for changes in behavior

### Red Flags to Watch For

⚠️ **Unusual behavior**:
- Spending excessive time on app
- Hiding screen when you approach
- Emotional changes after using app
- Withdrawal from real-life activities

⚠️ **Social concerns**:
- New "friends" asking for personal info
- Adults requesting private contact
- Requests for photos or personal details
- Pressure to keep secrets from parents

⚠️ **Content concerns**:
- Exposure to sexual or violent content
- Messages from unknown adults
- Requests to move to private messaging
- Inappropriate comments on posts

**If you notice these**: Contact Mudyin immediately (contact info below)
