## 7. MODERATION & APPEALS

### Moderation Process

1. Content is flagged (by user report or automated system)
2. Reviewed by trained moderator within 24 hours
3. Decision made: dismiss, warn, remove, or ban
4. You're notified of action and reason

### Appeal Your Removal/Ban

**You have 30 days to appeal**:
1. Go to your account settings
2. Find the removal/ban
3. Click "Appeal Decision"
4. Explain why you disagree
5. Submit

**Appeal review**:
- Different moderator reviews
- Can consider new information
- Takes up to 7 days
- Final decision made by admin

**Appeal decisions**:
- **Uphold**: Original decision stands
- **Overturn**: Action reversed, account restored
- **Modify**: Sentence reduced (e.g., 30 days → 7 days)

### You Can't Appeal

- If you received warning only
- If you're unbanned (already resolved)
- After 30 days (deadline passed)
