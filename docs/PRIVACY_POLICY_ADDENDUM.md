# Privacy Policy Addendum - Gallery & Community Features

**Effective Date**: February 2026
**Version**: 1.0

---

## Overview

This addendum supplements our main Privacy Policy and applies specifically to data collected and processed through the Gallery and Community sections of the Mudyin platform. Please read this alongside our main Privacy Policy.

## What Data We Collect

### Gallery Section

**Photos & Metadata**:
- Image file (stored on Cloudinary)
- Photo title, description, alt text
- Tags and categorization
- Upload date and time
- Your location (if you include in metadata - optional)
- Device information for uploads

**Engagement Data**:
- Who liked your photo
- Comments on photos
- Views and analytics
- Download statistics

**Example Data**: "John liked your photo at 2:45 PM on Feb 10"

### Community Section

**Posts & Comments**:
- Text content
- Images in posts
- Links shared
- Date/time posted
- Edit history (if modified)

**Social Graph**:
- Who you follow
- Who follows you
- Connected members
- Badges earned
- Program participation

**Engagement**:
- Likes on posts
- Comments written
- Shares/saves
- Notifications received

**Example Data**: "Jane followed you" or "2 people liked your post"

### Profile Information

**Visible Data**:
- Name (as you chose)
- Bio/about section
- Avatar/profile photo
- Badges and achievements
- Program(s) you're in
- Join date

**Hidden Data**:
- Email address (not public)
- Date of birth (for age verification)
- Real name (if different from display name)
- Contact information

## Who Can See Your Data

### Gallery Photos

**Public Photos** (Default):
- Anyone with Mudyin account
- Anonymous search engines (Google, Bing) - optional
- People you share link with

**Members-Only Photos**:
- Only verified Mudyin members
- Program facilitators
- Mudyin staff (if moderation needed)

**What Others Can See**:
- Photo, title, description, tags
- Your name and profile
- Comment count
- When posted
- Not your email or personal info

### Community Posts

**Public Posts** (Default):
- All Mudyin members
- Invited guests
- Search engines (optional)

**Members-Only Posts**:
- Verified members only
- Not visible in search
- Not shared externally

**Private Messages**:
- Only recipient sees content
- Moderators can view if reported
- Encrypted in transit

### Profiles

**Your Profile Visibility**:
- Name: Always visible (required)
- Bio: You control (public/members/private)
- Avatar: You control
- Badges: Always visible
- Program: You control
- Email: Private (never shared)

## Parental Controls (Under 13 Users)

### Parent/Guardian Access

If you're under 13, your parent/guardian can:

✅ **Can see**:
- Your posts and comments
- Your profile
- Who you're connected with
- Your activity (weekly summary)
- Reported safety concerns

❌ **Cannot see**:
- Private messages (unless reported)
- Photos others posted (only your uploads)
- Deleted content (once removed)

### Parent Dashboard

Your parent/guardian can access a dashboard showing:
- Last login time
- Posts created this month
- Followers/following count
- Activity summary
- Any reports or warnings
- Crisis situations (automatically alerted)

### Settings You Can Control

**At any age, you can**:
- Make profile private
- Turn off notifications
- Delete your own posts
- Block specific users
- Report harassment

**At under 13, parents control**:
- Deletion of account
- Privacy level (default members-only)
- Notification frequency

## Data Retention

### Active Content

**Your photos stay indefinitely** unless you:
- Delete them (immediate removal)
- Request account deletion (30-day grace period)
- Violate guidelines (removed after decision)

**Your posts stay indefinitely** unless you:
- Delete them (immediate removal)
- Request account deletion (removed after 30 days)

### Deleted Content

**When you delete**:
- Content removed from live site immediately
- Cached copies removed within 24 hours
- Search engines notified (removal from search)
- Backups retained for 90 days (technical purposes only)
- After 90 days: permanently deleted

**Important**: If others screenshot/save your content, we can't control that

### Account Deletion

**When you delete your account**:
- Profile immediately hidden
- Posts/photos archived (not viewable) for 30 days
- After 30 days: permanently deleted
- Email available for reuse after 30 days
- Cannot restore deleted account

**Note**: Comments on others' posts remain under "Deleted User"

### Moderation Data

**If content is flagged/removed**:
- Flagging report kept for 1 year
- Moderation decision kept indefinitely
- Your appeal kept with decision
- Used only for moderation purposes

## Data Security

### How We Protect Your Data

**In Transit**:
- HTTPS encryption (lock icon in browser)
- All connections secured
- Passwords hashed (never stored in readable form)

**At Rest**:
- Database encrypted
- Limited staff access (need permission)
- Regular security audits
- Automatic backups encrypted

**Passwords**:
- Never sent to your email
- Reset links expire after 1 hour
- Two-factor authentication available (recommended)

### What You Can Do

- Use strong password (8+ chars, numbers, symbols)
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Don't share password
- Log out on shared devices
- Report suspicious activity

## Data Sharing

### We Share With

✅ **Program Facilitators**:
- Basic profile info (for program tracking)
- Program milestones
- Aggregate participation data
- Only for program-related purposes

✅ **Safety Authorities** (If Needed):
- Content if illegal activity
- User info if child safety risk
- Limited to what's necessary
- Only with proper legal request

❌ **We DON'T Share With**:
- Advertisers or marketing companies
- Data brokers
- Third-party analytics (no tracking)
- Other users unless you approve
- Government (unless court order)

### Mudyin Staff Access

**Only when needed for**:
- Content moderation (flagged content)
- Safety concerns (harm to self/others)
- Support tickets you create
- Technical support (with your request)

**Our staff is bound by confidentiality**

## Your Rights

### Right to Access

**You can request**:
- Copy of all your data
- Account activity history
- Content you've created
- Who viewed your profile

**Request**: Go to Settings > Privacy > Download My Data

**Response**: Within 30 days

### Right to Correction

**You can**:
- Edit your profile anytime
- Update bio, avatar, program
- Correct personal information
- Change privacy settings

### Right to Deletion

**You can request**:
- Deletion of specific content
- Deletion of entire account
- Account deletion is final (30-day delay)

**Request**: Settings > Account > Delete Account

### Right to Data Portability

**You can export**:
- All your posts and comments
- All your photos
- Your profile information
- Follow/follower lists

**Format**: JSON or CSV

**Request**: Settings > Privacy > Export My Data

## Cookies & Tracking

### What We Use

**Essential Cookies**:
- Session cookies (logging you in)
- Security cookies (CSRF protection)
- Preference cookies (your settings)

**Analytics** (Optional):
- Page views (anonymized)

**Third-Party Cookies**:
- Cloudinary (image hosting) - has own privacy policy
- NextAuth.js (authentication) - session cookies only
- No tracking pixels or tag managers

**Do Not Track**: We respect your Do Not Track browser setting

## Glossary

- **HTTPS**: Encrypted internet connection
- **Hash/Hashing**: One-way encryption (password becomes unreadable)
- **Encryption**: Converting data to code (needs key to read)
- **CSRF**: Protection against fake form submissions
- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Extra login verification
