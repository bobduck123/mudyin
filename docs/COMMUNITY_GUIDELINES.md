# Mudyin Community Guidelines

**Last Updated**: February 2026
**Version**: 1.0
**Status**: Approved

---

## Our Commitment to Safety

The Mudyin Aboriginal Healing Centre is committed to creating a safe, respectful, and culturally sensitive community space where all members can share, learn, and heal together.

These Community Guidelines apply to all content posted on the Mudyin platform, including posts, photos, comments, stories, and messages.

## Core Values

1. **Respect**: We value and respect all community members, regardless of background
2. **Safety**: We prioritize the safety and wellbeing of all users, especially young people
3. **Cultural Respect**: We honor Indigenous cultures and sacred knowledge
4. **Accountability**: We take responsibility for our words and actions
5. **Support**: We support each other through challenges and celebrations

## What We Welcome

✅ **Positive Content**
- Sharing your journey and experiences in our programs
- Celebrating milestones and achievements
- Supporting and encouraging fellow members
- Asking questions and seeking advice
- Sharing cultural knowledge (with appropriate permissions)
- Photos from program activities and events
- Stories of transformation and healing

✅ **Safe Engagement**
- Respectful disagreements and different perspectives
- Constructive feedback
- Celebrating diversity and inclusion
- Supporting mental health and wellbeing discussions
- Sharing resources that help the community

## What We Don't Allow

❌ **Harmful Content**
- Content promoting or encouraging self-harm, suicide, or eating disorders
- Content glorifying violence or abuse
- Content targeting or bullying individuals or groups
- Sexual or adult content
- Content sexualizing minors in any way
- Content depicting violence or gore

❌ **Discriminatory Content**
- Hate speech targeting race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexuality, or disability
- Racist, sexist, homophobic, or transphobic slurs or language
- Conspiracy theories or misinformation
- Content dehumanizing any group of people

❌ **Illegal & Dangerous Content**
- Instructions for illegal activities
- Instructions for self-harm or dangerous activities
- Drug use promotion
- Weapons or explosives content
- Spam or commercial solicitation
- Phishing or scams

❌ **Privacy Violations**
- Sharing others' personal information without consent
- Recording or sharing conversations without consent
- Impersonation or fake accounts
- Doxxing (publishing personal information to harass)

❌ **Intellectual Property**
- Posting copyrighted material without permission
- Sharing Indigenous Cultural Intellectual Property without permission from Elders
- Plagiarism

## Our Moderation Process

### How We Detect Violations

1. **User Reports**: Community members can report inappropriate content
2. **Automated Detection**: AI-powered systems flag potentially harmful content
3. **Manual Review**: Our moderation team reviews flagged content
4. **Moderator Action**: Trained volunteer moderators take appropriate action

### Response Process

When content violates these guidelines, we may:

1. **Warning** (First offense): User receives a warning and learns about guidelines
2. **Content Removal** (Persistent violations): Content is removed from the platform
3. **Account Restrictions** (Repeated violations):
   - Temporary ban: 7-30 days
   - Permanent ban: Account termination
4. **Escalation**: Serious content reported to appropriate authorities

### Appeal Process

If your account is restricted or content removed, you can appeal within 30 days:

1. Go to your account settings
2. Click "Appeal Moderation Decision"
3. Explain why you believe the decision was wrong
4. Our moderation team will review within 7 days

## Specific Community Standards

### Age-Appropriate Content

**For users under 18:**
- No adult-only content (18+)
- No sexual or romantic content
- No graphic violence
- Limited access to sensitive mental health discussions

**For users 18+:**
- Access to full community
- Ability to view all content types
- Responsibility to respect younger members

### Mental Health Content

We support mental health discussions while keeping everyone safe:

**Allowed:**
- Sharing your mental health journey
- Asking for support
- Recommending mental health resources
- Discussing therapy or counseling

**Not Allowed:**
- Promoting self-harm or suicide
- Sharing content that could trigger vulnerable members
- Offering medical advice (only licensed professionals)
- Romanticizing mental illness

**Crisis Support**: If you or someone else is in crisis, call [Lifeline: 13 11 14](tel:131114)

### Program-Specific Rules

**YSMP (Youth Sexual Motivation Program)**:
- Celebrate milestones (50, 100, 365+ days)
- Share transformation stories
- Support other members' journeys
- No pressure or judgment

**Thrive Tribe**:
- Share healing journeys
- Support wellness practices
- Celebrate community growth
- Respect all healing approaches

**Healing Centre**:
- Honor Indigenous healing practices
- Respect Elder wisdom
- Protect sacred knowledge
- Credit and acknowledge sources

## Protecting Young People

This platform is a safe space for young people. We have special protections:

### For Under-13 Users
- Parental consent required
- Limited visibility of sensitive content
- No direct messaging with adults
- Extra moderation of their content
- Parent/guardian can access activity

### For All Young People (Under 18)
- No adult-only content recommendations
- Monitored for grooming or predatory behavior
- Protected from harassment
- Crisis resources available 24/7
- Report to eSafety Commissioner if needed

## Reporting Harmful Content

If you see content that violates these guidelines:

1. Click the "Report" button on the post/comment
2. Select the reason for your report
3. Add optional details about why you're reporting
4. Our team will review within 24 hours

**Serious Situations** (suicide, abuse, exploitation):
- Contact Lifeline: [13 11 14](tel:131114)
- Contact eSafety Commissioner: [esafety.gov.au](https://esafety.gov.au)
- Contact NSW Police: [000](tel:000)

## Consequences of Violations

**First Offense**:
- Automated warning
- Content may be removed
- Education message

**Repeated Violations**:
- Temporary account suspension (7-30 days)
- Content removal
- Communication from moderation team

**Severe Violations** (suicide, abuse, exploitation):
- Immediate account termination
- Report to authorities
- Communication with parents/guardians (if under 18)

**Permanent Ban** (3+ serious violations):
- Permanent removal from platform
- Cannot create new account with same person
- Report to authorities if illegal

## Cultural Respect & ICIP

This platform respects Indigenous Cultural Intellectual Property (ICIP).

**Sacred Knowledge**:
- Don't share sacred stories without permission from Elders
- Don't post photos of sacred sites without permission
- Acknowledge sources and communities
- Respect ceremony and cultural protocols

**Collaboration & Permission**:
- Ask before sharing others' content
- Credit community members
- Respect privacy of groups
- Follow Mudyin's cultural guidelines

## Accessibility

We're committed to making this platform accessible:

- **Screen readers**: All images have alt text
- **Captions**: Videos include captions
- **Color contrast**: Text meets accessibility standards
- **Keyboard navigation**: Full site is keyboard accessible
- **Readability**: Clear language and formatting

## Changes to These Guidelines

We may update these guidelines as the community grows. We'll notify members of significant changes.
